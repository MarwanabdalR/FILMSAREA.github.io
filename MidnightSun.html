    Dreams Come True at Night..
    Based on the Japanese film, Midnight Sun centers on Katie, a 17-year-old sheltered since childhood and confined to her house during the
    day with a life-threatening sensitivity to sunlight. This rare disease that makes even the smallest amount of sunlight deadly.
    Fate intervenes when she meets Charlie and they embark on a summer romance. Her world opens up after dark when she ventures out
    to play her guitar for random travelers. One night, Katie encounters Charlie, a young man she's secretly admired for years.
    As fate leads to a budding romance, Katie desperately tries to hide hercondition from her unsuspecting new beau.
