     Storyline

We need that touch from the one we love almost as much as we need air to breathe..
Seventeen-year-old Stella spends most of her time in the hospital as a cystic fibrosis patient. Her life is full of routines, boundaries and self-
control all of which get put to the test when she meets Will,an impossibly charming teen who has the same illness. There's an instant
flirtation, though restrictions dictate that they must maintain a safe distance between them. As their connection intensifies, so
does the temptation to throw the rules out the window and embrace that attraction. Love has no boundaries.
'Five Feet Apart'  is more than just a love story between Stella and Will...
