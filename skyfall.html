Skyfall

TRAILER

STORY

In Istanbul, MI6 agents James Bond and Eve Moneypenny pursue mercenary Patrice,
who has stolen a hard drive containing details of undercover agents. As Bond and Patrice
fight atop a moving train, M orders Moneypenny to shoot Patrice, despite not having a clear shot;
Moneypenny inadvertently hits Bond, who falls into a river. Bond is presumed dead and Patrice escapes
with the hard drive.
